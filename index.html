Welcome to the budget calculator!

This calculator is designed to calculate your daily budget based on the amount of money you have to use, how many days till the end of the month, how much you're hoping to save this month and the payments you still have to deliver (utilities, phone bill ect.)


Budgeting and handling money isn't easy. And more than often it creates more anxiety than gives you a peace of mind. This calculator is to be used as tool for assessing how much you could spend and afford in this month.


The equation is simple: bank balance minus savings minus costs and bills divided by the amount of days left in current month.
